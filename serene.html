FAQ

Find answers to commonly asked questions

How can I make a reservation?
You can make a reservation online through our website or by contacting our team directly. We make the process simple and easy for you.
What amenities does your resort offer?
We offer a range of amenities, including a pool, spa, fitness center, and on-site dining options. There’s something for everyone to enjoy.
Do you offer room service?
Yes, we offer room service for your convenience. You can enjoy a variety of delicious dishes from the comfort of your room.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of reservation you make. Please refer to our website or contact our team for more information.
Do you offer group bookings?
Yes, we offer group bookings for events or special occasions. Our team can work with you to create a customized package that meets your needs.
Is parking available at your resort?
Yes, we offer complimentary parking for our guests. You can park your vehicle with ease and enjoy your stay worry-free.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Our standard check-in time is 3:00 pm and check-out time is 12:00 pm. If you require early check-in or late check-out, please contact our team to make arrangements.
Do you offer wedding packages?
Yes, we offer wedding packages that include venue rental, catering, and accommodations for you and your guests. Our team can help bring your dream wedding to life.
